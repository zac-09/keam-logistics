What we offer

Customs Clearing

The firm undertakes all customs formalities for imports, transit shipments and export goods at all border points in Uganda, at the ports of Entebbe International Airport, Dar'esalaam and Mombasa. We represent our customers in customs clearance and other importation formalities.

Cargo Transport Management

We provide a variety of transport services for all types of cargo. Our large chain of associates in the transport business provides us with an edge over the rest. We guide our clients to select the most cost-effective and convenient shipping/transport method for their cargo.

Cargo Logistics advisory and Packaging

Whether it is the one time delivery you need done or a routine request, our team of cargo experts are always available to help you with any queries you might have. Our logistics processes, services, tools and systems in combination with strategic warehouse locations help our clients.
