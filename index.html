What We Do

Cargo Transport Management

We provide a variety of transport services for all types of cargo. Our large chain of associates in the transport business provides us with an edge over the rest. We guide our clients to select the most cost-effective and convenient shipping/transport method for their cargo.

Customs Clearing

The firm undertakes all customs formalities for imports, transit shipments and export goods at all border points in Uganda, at the ports of Entebbe International Airport, Daresalaam and Mombasa. We represent our customers in customs clearance and other importation formalities.

Cargo Logistics advisory and Packaging

Whether it is the one time delivery you need done or a routine request, our team of cargo experts are always available to help you with any queries you might have. Our logistics processes, services, tools and systems in combination with strategic warehouse locations help our clients.

Project logistics

We work and assist our clients to deal with all types of project cargo, containerized shipments, loose cargo (breakbulk), out of gauge shipments. We have a dedicated team with zeal and drive to work even under the tightest schedules to ensure that project execution timelines are complied with by ensuring that they deliver the cargo way in time for the project.

About us

KEAM Logistics Limited is freight, forwarding and Logistics Company limited by shares established in 2019.Following many years in the industry working for other large Clearing/ Forwarding and Shipping companies, the founders saw the need for a personalized and fast turn-around agency that focuses on customers. Since the company’s inception, we have been known for delivering reliable cargo handling services, both our client database and range of services have grown steadily. We strive to be Uganda’s fastest growing freight forwarding and transport company providing efficient and cost effective services to clients through quality processes.
